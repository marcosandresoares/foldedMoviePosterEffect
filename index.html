Akira (1988)

アキラ

Directed by Katsuhiro Otomo

A secret military project endangers Neo-Tokyo when it turns a biker gang member into a rampaging psychic psychopath who can only be stopped by two teenagers and a group of psychics.

Ghost in the Shell (1995)

攻殻機動隊

Directed by Mamoru Oshii

A cyborg policewoman and her partner hunt a mysterious and powerful hacker called the Puppet Master.

Paprika (2006)

パプリカ

Directed by Satoshi Kon

When a machine that allows therapists to enter their patients' dreams is stolen, all Hell breaks loose. Only a young female therapist, Paprika, can stop it.

Princess Mononoke (1997)

もののけ姫

Directed by Hayao Miyazaki

On a journey to find the cure for a Tatarigami's curse, Ashitaka finds himself in the middle of a war between the forest gods and Tatara, a mining colony. In this quest he also meets San, the Mononoke Hime.

Neon Genesis Evangelion:
The End of Evangelion (1997)

新世紀エヴァンゲリオン劇場版 Air/まごころを、君に

Directed by Hideaki Anno & Kazuya Tsurumaki

NERV face off against the Eighteenth Angel, humanity itself, but in the end the fate of the world depends upon Shinji's choices.
